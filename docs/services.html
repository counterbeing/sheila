Services

Therapy

Having spent 30 years working with couples in all stages of their relationship, she brings experience and insight to the challenges we face in developing and maintaining meaningful and satisfying relationships. Her work is focused on creating the kind of secure connection between partners which supports trust and intimacy. She also has extensive experience in teaching about and working with adolescent and individual adult development issues.

Coaching

Sometimes couples and individuals need assistance with communication skills, problem solving, or negotiation in relationships or professional settings. She has expertise in collaborative divorce negotiations and is trained in mediation. She also has assisted in custody negotiation and working through parent-child conflicts in numerous divorces.
